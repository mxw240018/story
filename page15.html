Whispy and her mouse have barely cleared the island when her hunting insticts alert her to live prey.
Just outside of the large window over by the dining table, there is....
"Wait here, mousey! I've gotta check this out!"
And there it is: a puff of feathers!
What a marvelous catch it would be, if only there wasn't this nearly transparent barrier between them.
As much as Whispy-Woo would LOVE to provide her humans with a proper snack, she loves her humans, big sister and cozy life too much to return to being an outdoor cat.
And the bird has no idea how fortunate it is.
